Frequently Asked Questions

Why scan your photos?

There are many answers to this.

Space Saving - Clear your cupboards and shelf space of printed photos and albums. Reduce your storage to the size of a USB

Family Sharing – There is usually only one copy of sentimental photos pre the digital age of photos. When you Digiscan your photos everyone can have copies. (Ask about our addition copy USB’s). Imagine the digital photo slide night you and your family can have with photos not seen in years!

Can you pick up and deliver?

Yes. Digiscan can pick up and deliver all over metropolitan Sydney.

How Long does scanning take?

Nearly all Digiscan jobs can be turned around in 48 hours.

Do my photos need to be sorted first?

No. At Digiscan we believe that scanning photos first is better. It is much quicker/easier to sort photos once they are digital from the comfort of your chair.

My photo albums are in a special order that I don't want to loose, what should I do?

We are the only expert photo scanners that place the photos back in the album in the same order no matter the orientation free of charge!

Only $40 per album?

We price our albums 50% below any competition while offering a greater service because we believe everyone should have the opportunity to enjoy their photos digitially?

Is the quality good?

At Digiscan we scan all photos at 600dpi (1200dpi available at extra cost) and also enhance all your photos to bring back the vibrant colors free of any additional price.
